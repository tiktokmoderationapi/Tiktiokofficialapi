Frequently Asked Questions

  • Can I block system messages?
    Yes. However, the system continues monitoring in the background. You simply won't receive messages anymore.
  • Is there a system account?
    No. TikTok operates the system directly – there's no user-visible account.
  • Are TikTok comments moderated?
    Not yet. Currently, the system only watches TikTok Live streams.
  • What languages are supported?
    English and German. More languages coming soon.
